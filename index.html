Photography is an emotion!

Hello!

I'm Pratibha Anugulia, a Computer Science Engineer by profession and a passionate photographer by heart. My journey into the world of photography began as a hobby, but over time, it has grown into a deep love and appreciation for the art.

Despite my background in engineering, I've always been drawn to the creative and expressive nature of photography. It's a field where I can apply my technical skills in a creative way, and I find that incredibly fulfilling.

I am currently exploring different genres of photography and constantly learning new techniques to improve my craft. The allure of capturing fleeting moments and transforming them into timeless memories was too enchanting to resist.I believe that there's always something new to learn in photography, and I'm excited about this journey of continuous growth and discovery.

Through this platform, I share my works as stock photos and provide valuable information about photography. If you're interested in my work or would like to learn more about photography, feel free to reach out to me. I'd love to connect with fellow photography enthusiasts and learners.

Thank you for ypur valuable time towards checking out my works. Here's to capturing the beauty of the world, one frame at a time! I look forward to sharing my journey with you and learning from all of you as well.
